n a secluded paradise caught between the sea and the sky, Nami Resort in Boracay Island provides an air of tranquility and an abundance of breathtaking views. Here, romantic gestures and relaxing activities are only complemented by the grandeur of nature. Its elevated location beside a cliff only kindles one’s affinity for adventure and exploration.

This Boracay villa resort has spacious rooms from which one can view Diniwid Beach and Sibuyan Sea in complete comfort. There are 3 accommodation types to choose from. The smallest, Deluxe Beach View, has an area of 35 sqm, while Suite Ocean View has 50 sqm, and One Bedroom Suite has 75 sqm. Each Boracay Island villa comes with a balcony, jacuzzi, LCD TV with cable channels, and wide glass windows that frame the verdant cliff and sapphire sea. Our guests only need to take the elevator to access the beach and Nami Resort’s deck, spa, and water sports facilities.

Our boutique resort presents personalized beach villa holidays. Our room service, bar, and restaurant offer a feast for the senses with panoramic views of the island and dishes and drinks that please the palate. The resort also has airport and city transfers, baby sitting service, and island adventure packages.
